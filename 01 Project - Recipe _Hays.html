Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

"This delicious recipe has been made ever since I was born and has been a hit for years and having everyone who tries it running back for more".

Ingredients

  • 3 Medium Bananas mashed
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or dressing
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. First preheat the oven to 350 dergees
  2. Grab a bowl and mix together mashed Bananas, Mayo and egg
  3. In a sperate bowl mix togther the dry ingredients of flour, salt,sugar,baking soda and chocolate chips
  4. Pour the batter into a pre greased loaf pan and cook for 60-70 minutes or until toothpick or fork comes out clean

Prep Time: 10 minutes, Cook Time 60-70 minutes

Serve warmly with a little drizzle of carmel for extra flavor, Enjoy!
